Q. In ΔABC below, AB = AC and x = 30. What is the value of y?

  1. 30

  2. 40

  3. 50

  4. 65

  5. 75

Solution:Since AB = AC, ΔABC is isosceles.
Hence, its base angles are equal: y = z. Since the angle sum of a triangle is 180˚, we get x + y + z = 180. Replacing z with y and x with 30 in this equation and then simplify ing yields

30 + y + y = 180

30 + 2y = 180

2y = 150

y = 75
The answer is E.
