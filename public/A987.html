Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

USURP : SEIZE ::

  1. foster : abash

  2. temper : modify

  3. cease : fetter

  4. indulge : forsake

  5. cite : manumit

Solution:USURP means to SEIZE (illegitimately).
Hence, we are looking for synonyms. Now, TEMPER means to MODIFY (think of the phrase tempered steel). The answer is (B).
