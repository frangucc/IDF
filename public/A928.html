Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PERFUNCTORY : SUPERLATIVE ::

  1. apparent : diffident

  2. daring : stalwart

  3. vivacious : corrupt

  4. problematic : austere

  5. reticent : brazen

Solution:PERFUNCTORY means done with little care, thoughtless, whereas SUPERLATIVE means excellent. Likewise, RETICENT (reserved) and BRAZEN (bold) are antonyms. The answer is (E).
