Q.   If p/19 is 1 less than 3 times q/19, then p equals which of the following expressions?

  1. 3q + 19

  2. 3q + 38

  3. 19/2

  4. 3q – 38

  5. 3q – 19

Solution:The clause "p/19 is 1 less than 3 times q/19" translates into:

 p 
 19 
 = 3 × 
 q 
 19 
 − 1

Multiplying both sides of this equation by 19 gives

p = 3 × q − 19
The answer is (E).
