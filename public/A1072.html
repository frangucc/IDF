Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Knowing Julian was overshadowed by many other actors, she knew she was indulging in a bit of _______ when she wondered whether Julian was the greatest living actor ever.

  1. irony

  2. overemphasis

  3. understatement

  4. hyperbole

  5. injustice

Solution:The statement implies that Julian is certainly not the greatest American actor. To believe him to be the greatest actor would be to indulge in "hyperbole." The answer is (D).
