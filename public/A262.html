Q. In the figure below, h denotes the height and b the base of the triangle. If (bh)2 = 16, what is the area of the triangle?

  1. 1

  2. 2

  3. 3

  4. 4

  5. Not enough information

Solution:Taking the square root of both sides of the equation (bh)2 = 16 gives

bh = 4

Plugging this into the area formula gives

Area = 
 1 
 2 
 × b × h = 
 1 
 2 
 × 4 = 2

Hence, the answer is B.
