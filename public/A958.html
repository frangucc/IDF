Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

TRAVESTY : PARAGON ::

  1. autonomy : subordination

  2. disqualification : ineptitude

  3. sentinel : creed

  4. conundrum : accountability

  5. progressiveness : refinement

Solution:TRAVESTY and PARAGON (perfection) are antonyms. Similarly, AUTONOMY (independence) and SUBORDINATION are antonyms. The answer is (A).
