Q.   What percent of 3x is 6y if x = 4y?

  1. 50%

  2. 40%

  3. 30%

  4. 20%

  5. 18%

Solution: Let z% represent the unknown percent. Now, when solving percent problems, "of" means times. Translating the statement "What percent of 3x is 6y" into an equation yields

z%(3x) = 6y

Substituting x = 4y into this equation yields

z%(3 × 4y) = 6y

     z%(12y) = 6y

              z% =
 6y 
 12y 

               z% = 1/2 = .50 = 50%
The answer is (A).
