Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MUTTER : INDISTINCT ::

  1. define : easy

  2. blunder : polished

  3. articulate : well-spoken

  4. expedite : completed

  5. censure : histrionic

Solution:There is no clear and necessary relationship between DEFINE and EASY. Defining something may or may not be easy. This eliminates (A). Now, to MUTTER is to speak INDISTINCTLY. Similarly, to ARTICULATE is to speak CLEARLY. The answer is (C).
(Note: In this problem the eye-catcher is the answer. Again, the eye-catcher can be the answer, though it rarely is.)
