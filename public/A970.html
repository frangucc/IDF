Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

RUSE : DECEIVE ::

  1. pretext : mollify

  2. invective : laud

  3. cathartic : cleanse

  4. artifice : disabuse

  5. calumny : confuse

Solution:The purpose of a RUSE (trick) is to DECEIVE. Similarly, the purpose of a CATHARTIC is to CLEANSE. The answer is (C).
