Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

CONTRITE : OBDURATE ::

  1. grievous : lamentable

  2. aphoristic : esoteric

  3. sophisticated : cultured

  4. verisimilar : apparent

  5. favorable : assenting

Solution:CONTRITE (sorry) and OBDURATE (hardened, shameless) are antonyms. Similarly, APHORISTIC (self evident) and ESOTERIC (obscure) are antonyms. The answer is (B).
