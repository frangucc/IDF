Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PERIPATETIC : MOTIONLESS ::

  1. lengthy : careless

  2. necessary : superfluous

  3. innocent : tedious

  4. vicarious : cooperative

  5. cultured : studious

Solution:PERIPATETIC means "pertaining to walking," which is the opposite of MOTIONLESS.
Hence, we are looking for antonyms. Now, NECESSARY and SUPERFLUOUS (excessive) are antonyms. The answer is (B).
