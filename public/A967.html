Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

ANARCHY : ORDER ::

  1. desolation : annihilation

  2. ineptitude : skill

  3. bastion : aegis

  4. chaos : disarray

  5. parsimony : elegance

Solution:ANARCHY means "a lack of ORDER, a state of chaos." Similarly, INEPTITUDE means "a lack of SKILL." The answer is (B).
