Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

The general accused the senator of naiveté for               that air strikes alone could stop the aggressors.

  1. advocating

  2. denying

  3. obfuscating

  4. mishandling

  5. disallowing

Solution:The general is accusing the senator of being naive (unsophisticated) for believing that air strikes alone could stop the aggressors. The answer is (A).
