Q.   If n > 2, then the sum, S, of the integers from 1 through n can be calculated by the following formula: S = n(n + 1)/2. Which one of the following statements about S must be true?

  1. S is always odd.

  2. S is always even.

  3. S must be a prime number.

  4. S must not be a prime number.

  5. S must be a perfect square.
