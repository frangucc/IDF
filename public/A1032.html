Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Liharev talks about being both a nihilist and an atheist during his life, yet he never does               faith in God.

  1. affirm

  2. lose

  3. scorn

  4. aver

  5. supplicate

Solution:"Yet" draws a contrast between what one would expect an Atheist to do (renounce faith in God) and what Liharev did (maintained faith in God). In other words, he did not lose faith in God. The answer is (B).
