Q.   If x is both the cube and the square of an integer and x is between 2 and 200, what is the value of x?

  1. 8

  2. 16

  3. 64

  4. 125

  5. 169

Solution:Since x is both a cube and between 2 and 200, we are looking at the integers:

23 , 33, 33, 43, 53

which reduce to

8, 27, 64, 125

There is only one perfect square, 64 = 82, in this set. The answer is (C).
