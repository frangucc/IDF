Q. In the figure below, which of the following points is three times as far from P as from Q?

  1. (0, 3)

  2. (1, 1)

  3. (4, 5)

  4. (2, 3)

  5. (4, 1)

Solution: From the distance formula, the distance between (4, 1) and Q is √2 , and the distance between (4, 1) and P is √(4 − 1)2 + (1 − 4)2 = √32 + (−3)2 
= √2 × 32 = 3√2
The answer is E.
