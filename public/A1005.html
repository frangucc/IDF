Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PERPETUAL : EVANESCENT ::

  1. irresolute : cryptic

  2. encouraging : venturesome

  3. blithe : disconsolate

  4. recondite : puzzling

  5. extrinsic : aberrant

Solution:PERPETUAL means continual, which is the opposite of EVANESCENT (fleeting). Similarly, BLITHE means blissful, which is the opposite of DISCONSOLATE (sad, gloomy). The answer is (C).
