Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

COPYRIGHT : BOOK ::

  1. letter : alphabet

  2. addendum : chapter

  3. cartoons : institution

  4. deed : property

  5. tool : measurement

Solution:There is no relationship between CARTOONS and INSTITUTION. Eliminate (C). Now, a COPYRIGHT identifies the owner of a BOOK. Similarly, a DEED identifies the owner of a PROPERTY. The answer is (D).
