Q.   A rancher is constructing a fence by stringing wire between posts 20 feet apart. If the fence is 400 feet long, how many posts must the rancher use?

  1. 18

  2. 19

  3. 20

  4. 21

  5. 22

Solution:Since the fence is 400 feet long and the posts are 20 feet apart, there are
 400 
 20 
 = 20 sections in the fence. Now, if we ignore the first post and associate the post at the end of each section with that section, then there are 20 posts (one for each of the twenty sections). Counting the first post gives a total of 21 posts. The answer is (D).
