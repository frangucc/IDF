Q.   If x and y are both prime and greater than 2, then which of the following CANNOT be a divisor of xy?

  1. 2

  2. 3

  3. 11

  4. 15

  5. 17

Solution:Since x and y are prime and greater than 2, xy is the product of two odd numbers and is therefore odd.
Hence, 2 cannot be a divisor of xy. The answer is (A).
