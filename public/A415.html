Q.   A jet uses 80 gallons of fuel to fly 320 miles. At this rate, how many gallons of fuel are needed for a 700 mile flight?

  1. 150

  2. 155

  3. 160

  4. 170

  5. 175

Solution:This is a direct proportion: as the distance increases, the gallons of fuel consumed also increases. Setting ratios equal yields

 80gal. 
 320mi. 
 = 
 xgal. 
 700mi. 

 700 × 80 
 320 
 = x

175 = x
The answer is (E).
