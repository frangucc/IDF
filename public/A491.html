Ques Refer the graph below to answer the question.

SALES BY CATEGORY FOR GRAMMERCY PRESS, 1980–1989

(in thousands of books)

Which of the following best approximates the amount by which the increase in sales of fiction titles from 1985 to 1986 exceeded the increase in sales of fiction titles from 1983 to 1984?

  1. 31.5 thousand

  2. 40 thousand

  3. 49.3 thousand

  4. 50.9 thousand

  5. 68 thousand

Solution:The graph shows that the increase in sales of fiction titles from 1985 to 1986 was approximately 40 thousand and the increase in sales of fiction titles from 1983 to 1984 was approximately 10 thousand.
Hence, the difference is

40 – 10 = 30

Choice (A) is the only answer-choice close to 30 thousand.
