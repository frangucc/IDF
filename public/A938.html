Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

CONSTELLATION : STARS ::

  1. amplifier : hearing

  2. ocean : water

  3. mosaic : tile

  4. tracks : train

  5. book : paper

Solution:A CONSTELLATION is made of STARS. Likewise, a MOSAIC is made of TILES. The answer is (C).

Choice (E) is perhaps second-best. A book is made of paper, but the pair BOOK : PAPER does not contain the notion of discrete units as does the pair MOSAIC : TILE.
