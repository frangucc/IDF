Q. Solve the following by checking for positive and negative connotations.

NOISOME:

  1. salubrious

  2. affable

  3. multifarious

  4. provident

  5. officious

Solution:NOISOME is a very negative word, so we are looking for a very positive word. Now, "multifarious" is neutral: it means "diverse, many-sided." "Provident" is a positive synonym for "miserly." "Officious" is negative: it means "acting like an official, sticking your nose into other people’s business." Finally, both "salubrious" and "affable" are positive, but "salubrious" (healthful) is more positive. So (A) is the answer. NOISOME means "noxious."
