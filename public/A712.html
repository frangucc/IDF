Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

BLASÉ:

  1. satiated

  2. humdrum

  3. provoked

  4. jovial

  5. robust

Solution:BLASÉ is a French word, which means to sicken. The meaning of the word is to become world-weary or apathetic to pleasure or excitement. PROVOKED means to arouse or provide stimulation. The answer is (C).
