Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

FLAG : STRENGTH ::

  1. excite : activity

  2. demote : rank

  3. confide : modesty

  4. doff : malformation

  5. estrange : breach

Solution:To FLAG is to lose STRENGTH. Similarly, to DEMOTE is to lower in RANKThe answer is (B).
