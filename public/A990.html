Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

INDISTINCT : PECULIARITY ::

  1. ingenuous : deception

  2. abhorrent : odium

  3. prosaic : indifference

  4. depraved : trepidation

  5. inchoate : exhilaration

Solution:INDISTINCT means ordinary, without any PECULIAR traits. Similarly, INGENUOUS means without DECEPTION, innocent. The answer is (A).
