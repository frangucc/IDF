Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SHOWER : CLOUDBURST ::

  1. wave : storm

  2. sound : noise

  3. censure : admonishment

  4. shard : pottery

  5. recession : depression

Solution:A CLOUDBURST is a heavy downpour. So it is much more intense than a SHOWER. Similarly, a DEPRESSION is a severe RECESSION. The answer is (E).

Watch out for (C). Although CENSURE is a severe form of ADMONISHMENT, the direction is wrong: an ADMONISHMENT is less intense, not more intense, than a CENSURE.
