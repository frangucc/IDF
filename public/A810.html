Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

ABERRANT:

  1. catholic

  2. euphoric

  3. customary

  4. lax

  5. putrid

Solution:The root "err" means "to wander," and the prefix "ab" means "away from."
Hence, to wander away from. In this case, to wander away from the normal. The opposite is CUSTOMARY (common). The answer is (C).
