Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MALIGNANT : INNOCUOUS ::

  1. pretentious : haughty

  2. nonplused : vile

  3. spurious : indubitable

  4. discreet : astute

  5. assenting : sympathetic

Solution:MALIGNANT means "injurious, evil," and INNOCUOUS means "harmless. "
Hence, we are looking for antonyms. Now, SPURIOUS means "false, deceptive" whereas INDUBITABLE means "certain, sure. "
Hence, the answer is (C).
