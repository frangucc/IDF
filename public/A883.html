Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

ANARCHY : GOVERNMENT ::

  1. confederation : state

  2. trepidation : courage

  3. serenity : equanimity

  4. surfeit : food

  5. computer : harddrive

Solution:Paraphrase: ANARCHY is the lack of GOVERNMENT. Now, a CONFEDERATION does not lack STATES. However, TREPIDATION is the lack of COURAGE. The answer is (B).
