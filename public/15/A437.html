Q.   If 3y + 5 = 7x, then 21y – 49x =

  1. –40

  2. –35

  3. –10

  4. 0

  5. 15

Solution:First, interchanging 5 and 7x in the expression 3y + 5 = 7x yields 3y – 7x = –5.
Next, factoring
21y – 49x yields

21y – 49x =

7.3y − 7.7x =

7(3y – 7x) =

7(–5) = since 3y – 7x = –5

–35
The answer is (B).
