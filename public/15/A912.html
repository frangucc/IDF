Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DILIGENT : ASSIDUOUS ::

  1. suspicious : reliable

  2. cautious : indecisive

  3. repentant : innocent

  4. peerless : common

  5. indigent : poor

Solution:DILIGENT and ASSIDUOUS are synonyms, both mean "hardworking. "Likewise, INDIGENT and POOR are synonyms. The answer is (E).
