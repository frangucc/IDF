Q.   1/2 of 0.2 percent equals

  1. 1

  2. 0.1

  3. 0.01

  4. 0.001

  5. 0.0001

Solution: Recall that percent means to divide by 100. So .2 percent equals .2/100 = .002. (Recall that the decimal point is moved to the left one space for each zero in the denominator.) Now, as a decimal 1/2 = .5.

In percent problems, "of" means multiplication. So multiplying .5 and .002 yields

.002 × .5 = 0.0001

Hence, the answer is (D).
