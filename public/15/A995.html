Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

REFINED : AIRS ::

  1. proper : prudish

  2. boastful : timidity

  3. didactic : incertitude

  4. grievous : furtherance

  5. lackluster : siren

Solution:AIRS means REFINED, but it"s negative. Similarly, PRUDISH means PROPER, but it"s negative. The answer is (A).
