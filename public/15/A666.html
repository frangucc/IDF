Q. Choose the word most opposite in meaning to the capitalized word.

METTLE:

  1. ad hoc

  2. perdition

  3. woe

  4. trepidation

  5. apathy

Solution:Think of the phrase: "To test your mettle." (The large waves tested the surfer’s mettle.) METTLE means "character, courage." The opposite is TREPIDATION, which means fear. The answer is (D).
