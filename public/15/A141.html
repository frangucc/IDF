Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x = 3y and x > y.

Column A
x + y

Column B
8

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: If x = 9 and y = 3, then x = 3y is satisfied (9 = 3 × 3) and x > y is also satisfied (9 > 3). In this case, x + y = 9 + 3 = 12 > 8. However, if x = 3 and y = 1, then x = 3y is again satisfied (3 = 3 × 1) and x > y is also satisfied (3 > 1). In this case, x + y = 3 + 1 = 4 < 8. The answer is (D).
