Q. Look for secondary meanings; then solve.

STEEP:

  1. desiccate

  2. intensify

  3. pontificate

  4. whet

  5. hamper

Solution:As an adjective, STEEP means "precipitous." But as a verb, STEEP means "to saturate." Think of the phrase, "Steeped in tradition." In other words, filled with tradition. The opposite is to dry up, which is the meaning of desiccate. The answer is (A).
