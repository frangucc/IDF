Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

APOLOGIZE : PENITENT ::

  1. contravene : dishonorable

  2. enjoin : merciful

  3. smite : wrathful

  4. underwrite : provisional

  5. embrace : woeful

Solution:To APOLOGIZE is an act of a PENITENT person. Similarly, to SMITE (attack, torment) is an act of a WRATHFUL person. The answer is (C).
