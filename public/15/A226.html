Q. In the figure below, if O is the center of the circle, then y =

  1. 75

  2. 76

  3. 77

  4. 78

  5. 79

Solution:OS and OT are equal since they are radii of the circle.
Hence, ΔSOT is isosceles.
Therefore, S = T = 51˚. Recalling that the angle sum of a triangle is 180˚, we get S + T + y = 51˚ + 51˚ + y = 180˚. Solving for y gives y = 78˚. The answer is D.
