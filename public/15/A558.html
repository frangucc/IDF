Q.   The median is larger than the average for which one of the following sets of integers?

  1. {8, 9, 10, 11, 12}

  2. {8, 9, 10, 11, 13}

  3. {8, 10, 10, 10, 12}

  4. {10, 10, 10, 10, 10}

  5. {7, 9, 10, 11, 12}

Solution:The median in all five answer-choices is 10. By symmetry, the average in answer-choices (A), (C), and (D) is 10 as well. The average in choice (B) is larger than 10 because 13 is further away from 10 than 8 is. Similarly, the average in choice (E) is smaller than 10 because 7 is further away from 10 than 12 is. The exact average is
 7 + 9 + 10 + 11 + 12 
 5 
 = 
 49 
 5 
 < 10. The answer is (E).
