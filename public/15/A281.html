Q. The value of x + y + z =

  1. 120˚

  2. 160˚

  3. 180˚

  4. 270˚

  5. 360˚

Solution:Since angles A, B, and C are the interior angles of the triangle, their angle sum is 180˚.
Hence, A + B + C = 180. Since A and y are vertical angles, they are equal. This is also true for angles B and z and angles C and x. Substituting these values into the equation yields y + z + x = 180. The answer is C.
