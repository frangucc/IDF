Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

ILLUMINATE : ADUMBRATE ::

  1. delimit : prostrate

  2. misconceive : civilize

  3. lucid : dilatory

  4. patronize : consummate

  5. inculpate : vindicate

Solution:ILLUMINATE means to make clear, to enlighten, which is the opposite of ADUMBRATE (to darken, to obscure). Similarly, INCULPATE means to accuse, which is the opposite of VINDICATE. The answer is (E).
