Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x is a positive integer.

Column A
The number of distinct prime factors of x

Column B
The number of distinct prime factors of x3

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: We need only look at x = 1, 2, and 3. If x = 1, then x has no prime factors, likewise for x3.
Next, if x = 2, then x has one prime factor, 2, and x3 = 23 = 8 also has one prime factor, 2. Finally, if x = 3, then x has one prime factor, 3, and x3 = 23 = 8 also has one prime factor, 3. In all three cases, the columns are equal.
Hence, the answer is (C).
