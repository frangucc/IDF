Q. Solve the following problems by using substitution.

Suppose x is divisible by 8 but not by 3. Then which of the following CANNOT be an integer?

  1. x/2

  2. x/4

  3. x/6

  4. x/8

  5. x

Solution:Suppose x = 8. Then x is divisible by 8 and is not divisible by 3. Now, x/2 = 4, x/4 = 2, x/8 = 1, and x = 8, which are all integers—eliminate (A), (B), (D), and (E).
Hence, by process of elimination, the answer is (C).
