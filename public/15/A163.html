Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Column A
The unit’s digit of 610

Column B
The unit’s digit of 511

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: The unit’s digit of the number 6 raised to any positive integer power is always 6 (multiply out a few examples until you are convinced). The unit’s digit of the number 5 raised to any positive integer power is always 5 (multiply out a few examples until you are convinced).
Hence, Column A is larger, and the answer is (A).
