Q. In the following sentence, part or all of the sentence is underlined. The answer-choices offer five ways of phrasing the underlined part. If you think the sentence as written is better than the alternatives, choose A, which merely repeats the underlined part; otherwise choose one of the alternatives.

Regarding legalization of drugs, I am not concerned so much by its potential impact on middle class America but instead by its potential impact on the inner city.

  1. but instead

  2. so much as

  3. rather

  4. but rather

  5. as

Solution:The correct structure for this type of sentence is not so much by _____ as by _____. The answer is (E).
