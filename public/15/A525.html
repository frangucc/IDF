Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Column A
The time required to travel d miles at s miles per hour

Column B
The time required to travel 2d miles at 2s miles per hour

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution:The time required to travel d miles at s miles per hour is

distance traveled / speed = d/s

The time required to travel 2d miles at 2s miles per hour is

distance traveled / speed = 2d/2s = d/s

Hence, the time taken for either journey is the same. The answer is (C).
