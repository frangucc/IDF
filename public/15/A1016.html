Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MALINGER : ILLNESS ::

  1. masquerade : identity

  2. flag : strength

  3. disguise : mask

  4. vacillate : influenza

  5. skulk : abatement

Solution:To MALINGER is to fake an ILLNESS. Similarly, to MASQUERADE is to fake an IDENTITY. The answer is (A).
