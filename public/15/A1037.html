Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Though most explicitly sexist words have been replaced by gender-neutral terms, sexism thrives in the               of many words.

  1. indistinctness

  2. similitude

  3. loquacity

  4. implications

  5. obscurity

Solution:The sentence is saying that although a word may not be explicitly sexist it may contain sexist connotations or implications. The answer is (D).
