Q. In the figure below, the value of a + b is

  1. 118

  2. 119

  3. 120

  4. 121

  5. 122

Solution:Since the two horizontal lines are parallel (Why?), angle a and the angle with measure 29 are alternate interior angles and therefore are equal. Further, from the drawing, angle b is 90˚.
Hence, a + b = 29 + 90 = 119. The answer is B.
