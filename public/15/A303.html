Q. In the figure below, which of the following points lies within the circle?

  1. (3.5, 9.5)

  2. (–7, 7)

  3. (–10, 1)

  4. (0, 11)

  5. (5.5, 8.5)

Solution: For a point to be within a circle, its distance from the center of the circle must be less than the radius of the circle. The distance from (6, 8) to (0, 0) is the radius of the circle: R = √(6 − 0)2 + (8 − 0)2 = √36 + 64 = √100 = 10.

Now, let’s calculate the distance between (–7, 7) and (0, 0)

R = √(−7 − 0)2 + (7 − 0)2 = √49 + 49 = √98 < 0

The answer is B.
