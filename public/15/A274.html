Q. Two squares each with sides of length s are joined to form a rectangle. The area of the rectangle is

  1. s2

  2. 2s2

  3. 4s2

  4. 8s2

  5. 16s2

Solution:The area of a square with side s is s2. On joining two such squares, the resulting area will be twice the area of either square: 2s2. The answer is B.
