Q. Look for secondary meanings; then solve.

TABLE:

  1. palliate

  2. acclimate

  3. garner

  4. propound

  5. expedite

Solution:None of the answer-choices are nouns, so TABLE cannot be referring to furniture. As a verb, TABLE means "to postpone." You may have heard it used in government: "Congress tabled the bill." The opposite is to expedite. The answer is (E).

Choice (D), "propound," is second-best. However, "expedite" is more precisely opposite because it includes the notion of speeding up the consideration of a proposal.
