EXPONENTS

Exponents afford a convenient way of expressing long products of the same number. The expression bn is called a power and it stands for b × b × b × â€¦ × b, where there are n factors of b. b is called the base, and n is called the exponent. By definition, b0 = 1

There are six rules that govern the behavior of exponents:

Rule 1:
xa × xb = xa+b
Example, 23 × 22 = 23+2 = 25 = 32.
Caution, xa + xb ≠ xa+b

(xa)b = xab
