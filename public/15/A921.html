Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

ACCOMMODATING : SERVILE ::

  1. axiomatic : treacherous

  2. prudent : harmonious

  3. virile : feckless

  4. conformable : gross

  5. optimistic : sanguine

Solution:ACCOMMODATING and SERVILE are synonyms"though servile is not a complimentary term. Similarly, OPTIMISTIC and SANGUINE are synonyms. The answer is (E).
