Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

This argues well that Erikson exercised less free will than Warner; for even though Erikson was aware that he was misdirected, he was still unable to               free will.

  1. defer

  2. facilitate

  3. proscribe

  4. prevent

  5. exert

Solution:The sentence implies that even when Erikson knows he is taking the wrong path in life, he still cannot stop. That is, he cannot exert free will. The answer is (E).
