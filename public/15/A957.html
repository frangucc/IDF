Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

NOCTURNAL : CIMMERIAN ::

  1. exacting : lax

  2. prudish : indulgent

  3. contentious : affluent

  4. stark : embellished

  5. specious : illusory

Solution:Both NOCTURNAL and CIMMERIAN mean "dark, nightly. "
Hence, we are looking for synonyms. Now, both SPECIOUS and ILLUSORY mean "deceptive, false. "
Hence, the answer is (E).
