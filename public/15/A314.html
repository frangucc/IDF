Q. After being marked down 20 percent, a calculator sells for $10. The original selling price was

  1. $20

  2. $12.5

  3. $12

  4. $9

  5. $7

Solution: Twenty dollars is too large. The discount was only 20 percent eliminate (A). Both (D) and (E) are impossible since they are less than the selling price eliminate. 12 is the eye-catcher: 20% of 10 is 2 and 10 + 2 = 12. This is too easy for a hard problem eliminate. Thus, by process of elimination, the answer is B.
