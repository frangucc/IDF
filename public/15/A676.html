Q. Change the capitalized word into a more common form of the word and then find its antonym.

FANATICISM:

  1. delusion

  2. fascism

  3. remorse

  4. cynicism

  5. indifference

Solution:FANATICISM contains FANATIC which in turn contains FAN. Now, at a sporting event, fans often become overenthusiastic, which is precisely the meaning of FANATIC. Thus, we are looking for a word that means unenthusiastic. That is the meaning of INDIFFERENCE. The answer is (E).
