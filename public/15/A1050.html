Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

The interviewer was startled to hear the otherwise gracious author make the               remark: "My novels are too sophisticated for the American public."

  1. apt

  2. enigmatic

  3. lofty

  4. vacuous

  5. insightful

Solution:We are told that the author is gracious, yet she makes the churlish comment: "My novels are to sophisticated for the American public." Such an out of character comment is enigmatic. The answer is (B).
