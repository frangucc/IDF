Q. Solve the following problems by using substitution.

If both x and y are positive even integers, then which of the following expressions must also be even?

I. yx − 1
II. y – 1
III. x/2

  1. I only

  2. II only

  3. III only

  4. I and III only

  5. I, II, and III

Solution:If x = y = 2, then yx − 1 = 22 − 1 = 21= 2, which is even. But y – 1 = 2 – 1 = 1 is odd, and x/2 = 2/2 = 1 is also odd. This eliminates choices (B), (C), (D), and (E). The answer is (A).
