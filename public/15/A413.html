Q.   The ratio of two numbers is 10 and their difference is 18. What is the value of the smaller number?

  1. 2

  2. 5

  3. 10

  4. 21

  5. 27

Solution:Let x and y denote the numbers. Then x/y = 10 and x – y = 18. Solving the first equation for x and plugging it into the second equation yields

10y – y = 18

9y = 18

y = 2

Plugging this into the equation x – y = 18 yields x = 20.
Hence, y is the smaller number. The answer is (A).
