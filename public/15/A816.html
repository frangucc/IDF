Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

ASTRINGENT:

  1. harsh

  2. lax

  3. engaging

  4. incredulous

  5. pusillanimous

Solution:The root "string" means "binding, strict."
Hence, ASTRINGENT means "harsh, binding.". The opposite is LAX (loose). The answer is (B).
