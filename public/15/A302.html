Q. In the figure below, which of the following could be the coordinates of a point in the shaded region?

  1. (1, 2)

  2. (–2, 3)

  3. (3, –5)

  4. (–5, 1)

  5. (–1, –6)

Solution: The shaded region is entirely within the third quadrant. Now, both coordinates of any point in Quadrant III are negative. The only point listed with both coordinates negative is
(–1, –6). The answer is E.
