Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Though a small man, J. Egar Hoover appeared to be much larger behind his desk; for, having skillfully designed his office, he was               by the perspective.

  1. augmented

  2. comforted

  3. apprehended

  4. lessened

  5. disconcerted

Solution:The passage states that when sitting behind his desk J. Egar Hoover looked larger than he actually was. So the perspective must have increased the appearance of his size. The only word that means to increase is "augmented." The answer is (A).
