Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

OMNISCIENT : KNOWLEDGE ::

  1. saturnine : energy

  2. complete : retraction

  3. principled : method

  4. inquisitive : science

  5. boundless : expanse

Solution:OMNISCIENT means all-knowing, unlimited KNOWLEDGE. Similarly, BOUNDLESS means unlimited EXPANSE. (The expanse of the universe is boundless.) The answer is (E).
