Q. Change the capitalized word into a more common form of the word and then find its antonym.

INTERMINABLE:

  1. finite

  2. jejune

  3. tranquil

  4. incessant

  5. imprudent

Solution:INTERMINABLE comes from the base word TERMINATE—to stop. Now, the prefix in means "not," so INTERMINABLE means "not able to stop." The only word that contains the notion of stopping or limitedness is FINITE.
Hence, the answer is (A).
