Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Though he claimed the business was               , his irritability               that claim.

  1. sound . . belied

  2. expanding . . supported

  3. downsizing . . vindicated

Solution:If the business was not sound, his irritability would belie (contradict) his claim that the busi ness was sound. The answer is (A).
