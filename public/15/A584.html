Q.   If 813 = 2z, then z =

  1. 10

  2. 13

  3. 19

  4. 26

  5. 39

Solution:The number 8 can be written as 23. Plugging this into the equation 813 = 2zyields

(23)13 = 2z

Applying the rule (xa)b = xabyields

239 = 2z

Since the bases are the same, the exponents must be the same.
Hence, z = 39, and the answer is (E).
