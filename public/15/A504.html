DISTRIBUTION OF IMPORTS AND EXPORTS FOR COUNTRY X IN 1994.

Imports: 200 million items

Exports: 100 million items

In 1994, if twice as many autos imported to Country X broke down as autos exported from Country X and 20 percent of the exported autos broke down, what percent of the imported autos broke down?

  1. 1%

  2. 1.5%

  3. 2%

  4. 4%

  5. 5.5%

Solution: If 20% of the exports broke down, then 2 million autos broke down (20%x10). Since "twice as many autos imported to Country X broke down as autos exported from Country X," 4 million imported autos broke down. Further, Country X imported 100 million autos (50%x200). Forming the percentage yields
 4 
 100 
 = 0.04 = 4% The answer is (D).
