Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DWINDLE : SIZE ::

  1. sprint : emotion

  2. subpoena : annexation

  3. lynch : wantonness

  4. declaim : logic

  5. dissipate : strength

Solution:To DWINDLE is to decrease in SIZE. Similarly, to DISSIPATE is to decrease in STRENGTH. The answer is (E).
