Q.   Two cars start at the same point and travel in opposite directions. If one car travels at 45 miles per hour and the other at 60 miles per hour, how much time will pass before they are 210 miles apart?

  1. 5 hours

  2. 1 hour

  3. 1.5 hours

  4. 2 hours

  5. 2.5 hours

Solution:Since the cars start at the same time, the time each has traveled is the same. Let t be the time when the cars are 210 miles apart. The equation D = R × T , yields

210 = 45 × t + 60 × t

210 = 105 × t

2 = t
The answer is (D).
