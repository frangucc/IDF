The amount of profit made from government copy sales in 1992 was

  1. 70 thousand

  2. 100 thousand

  3. 150 thousand

  4. 200 thousand

  5. 350 thousand

Solution:The Total Profit graph shows that Zippy Printing earned about $340,000 from copying in 1992. The Pie Chart indicates that 20% of this was earned from government sales. Multiplying these numbers gives

$340,000 × 20% ≈ $70,000
The answer is (A).
