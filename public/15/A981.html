Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

UNWITTING : ACCIDENT ::

  1. helpful : succor

  2. tractable : recidivism

  3. abstruse : simplicity

  4. compensatory : gratis

  5. meticulous : duty

Solution:UNWITTING (unknowing) means caused by ACCIDENT. Similarly, SUCCOR means anything that is HELPFUL. The answer is (A).
