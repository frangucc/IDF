Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Because of his success as a comedian, directors were loath to consider him for               roles.

  1. supporting

  2. leading

  3. dramatic

  4. comedic

  5. musical

Solution:If the public expects a comedian to always make them laugh, then they might not accept a comedian in a serious role.
Hence, the directors would be loath (reluctant) to cast a comedian in a dramatic role. The answer is (C).
