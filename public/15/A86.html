Q.   Which one of the following could be the difference between two numbers both of which are divisible by 2, 3 and 4?

  1. 71

  2. 72

  3. 73

  4. 74

  5. 75

Solution:A number divisible by all three numbers 2, 3, and 4 is also divisible by 12.
Hence, each number can be written as a multiple of 12. Let the first number be represented as 12a and the second number as 12b. Assuming a > b, the difference between the two numbers is 12a – 12b = 12(a – b). Observe that this number is also a multiple of 12.
Hence, the answer must also be divisible by 12. Since 72 is the only answer-choice divisible by 12, the answer is (B).
