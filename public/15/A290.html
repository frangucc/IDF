Q. In the figure, the ratio of x to y is 2. What is the value of y ?

  1. 108

  2. 90

  3. 68

  4. 45

  5. 36

Solution: Since the ratio of x to y is 2, we get x/y = 2. Solving this equation for x yields x = 2y. Since the sum of the angles made by a line is 180˚, y + x + y = 180. Substituting 2y for x in this equation yields

y + 2y + y = 180

            4y = 180

              y = 45
The answer is D.
