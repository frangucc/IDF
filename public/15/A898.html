Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SPEECH : FILIBUSTER ::

  1. race : marathon

  2. gift : breach

  3. statement : digression

  4. detour : path

  5. address : postage

Solution:ADDRESS is an eye-catcher since it might remind one of a SPEECH. DIGRESSION is also an eye-catcher since one may digress during a SPEECH. Now, a FILIBUSTER is a long SPEECH. Similarly, a MARATHON is a long RACE. The answer is (A).
