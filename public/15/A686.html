Q. Solve the following by checking for positive and negative connotations.

CAJOLE:

  1. implore

  2. glance at

  3. belittle

  4. ennoble

  5. engender

Solution:CAJOLE has a positive connotation. "Implore," "ennoble," and "engender" are all neutral to positive, and they are all similar to CAJOLE—eliminate. "Glance at" is neutral—eliminate. Thus, by process of elimination, the answer is (C), "belittle." CAJOLE means "to encourage."
