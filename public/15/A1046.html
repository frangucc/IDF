Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

The citizenry had become so                by the president’s                that the latest financial scandal did not even make the front page of the newspapers.

  1. fascinated . . impropriety

  2. disgusted . . peccadilloes

  3. distraught . . magnanimity

  4. regretful . . personification

  5. jaded . . indiscretions

Solution:A financial scandal is an indiscretion; and it may not have made the front page because the public was jaded (worn out) by an excess of scandals. The answer is (E).
