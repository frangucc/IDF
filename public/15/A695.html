Q. Eliminate the eye-catchers in the following; then solve.

WHET:

  1. obscure

  2. blunt

  3. desiccate

  4. imbibe

  5. enervate

Solution:The eye-catcher is "desiccate": the opposite of "wet." However, WHET has nothing to do with water. In fact, WHET means "to stimulate." Think of the saying "To whet your appetite." The opposite of stimulating a desire or emotion is dulling or blunting it. The answer is (B).
