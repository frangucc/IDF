Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

AROMATIC : SMELL ::

  1. debonair : grandeur

  2. tart : taste

  3. intimate : notification

  4. unctuous : antithesis

  5. unsavory : vision

Solution:AROMATIC means having a strong SMELL. Similarly, TART means having a strong TASTE. The answer is (B).
