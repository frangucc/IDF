Q. In the figure, the equation of line AB is y = x + 2. The difference of the x- and y-coordinates of any point on the line is equal to:

  1. –4

  2. –2

  3. 0

  4. 2

  5. 4

Solution: Since the coordinates x and y are on the line, we know that y = x + 2.
Hence, the difference of x and y is

x – y = x – (x + 2) = –2
The answer is B.
