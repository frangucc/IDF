Q. Which one of the following could be the graph of 3 − 6x ≤ 
 4x + 2 
 −2 
?

Solution: Multiplying both sides of the inequality by –2 yields –2(3 – 6x) ≥ 4x + 2

Distributing the –2 yields –6 + 12x ≥ 4x + 2

Subtracting 4x and adding 6 to both sides yields 8x ≥ 8

Dividing both sides of the inequality by 8 yields x ≥ 1
The answer is D.
