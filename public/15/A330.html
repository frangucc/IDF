Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x4y < 0
xy4 > 0

Column A
x

Column B
y

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: Since x is raised to an even exponent, it is greater than or equal to zero. Further, since x4y ≠ 0, we know that neither x nor y is zero (otherwise x4y = 0).
Hence, we may divide x4y < 0 by x4 without reversing the inequality:

 x4y 
 x4 
 < 
 0 
 x4 

Simplifying yields y < 0

A similar analysis of the inequality xy4 > 0 shows that x > 0.
Hence, Column A is larger than Column B. The answer is A.
