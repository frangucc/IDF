Q. In the figure, what is the value of x?

  1. 20˚

  2. 30˚

  3. 40˚

  4. 50˚

  5. 60˚

Solution:In the figure, ∠B is the sum of ∠ABD and ∠DBC. So, ∠B = ∠ABD + ∠DBC = 40 + 40 = 80. Now, recall that the sum of the angles in a triangle is 180˚.
Hence,

∠A + ∠B + ∠C = 180

50 + 80 + x = 180 since ∠A = 50 and ∠B = 80

130 + x = 180

x = 50
The answer is D.
