Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

CONSANGUINITY:

  1. estrangement

  2. asylum

  3. resolve

  4. fraternity

  5. pantomime

Solution:The prefix "con" means "together," and the root "sangui" means "blood."
Hence, CONSANGUINITY means "related by blood," and by extension it means close relation or affinity. The opposite is ESTRANGEMENT. The answer is (A).
