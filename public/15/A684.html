Q. Solve the following by checking for positive and negative connotations.

DERISION:

  1. urgency

  2. admonishment

  3. uniqueness

  4. diversity

  5. acclaim

Solution:Since DERISION starts with DE, it should be negative. So we are looking for a positive word. "Urgency" and "admonishment" are both somewhat negative—eliminate. "Uniqueness" and "diversity" are both neutral—eliminate.
Hence, by process of elimination, the answer is (E), "acclaim." DERISION means "scorn."
