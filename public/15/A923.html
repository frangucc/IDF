Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

OSTRICH : BIRD ::

  1. dusk : day

  2. fish : ocean

  3. tunnel : mountain

  4. hat : coat

  5. sirocco : storm

Solution:An OSTRICH is a type of BIRD. Likewise, a SIROCCO is a type of STORM"a dust storm. The answer is (E).

Note, even without knowing the meaning of SIROCCO, you can still solve this analogy: DUSK is not a type of DAY, FISH is not a type of OCEAN, TUNNEL is not a type of MOUNTAIN, and finally HAT is not a type of COAT.
Hence, by the process of elimination, the answer is (E).
