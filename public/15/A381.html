Q.   If s + S ≠ 0 and
 1

 = 
 1 
 4 
 s − S 
 s + S 
, then what is s in terms of S ?

  1. s = S + 3

  2. s = 4S

  3. s = S/12

  4. s = –7S

  5. s = 4S – 6

Solution:First, clear fractions by multiplying both sides by 12(s + S): 4(s + S) = 3(s – S)

Next, distribute the 3 and the 4: 4s + 4S = 3s – 3S

Finally, subtract 3s and 4S from both sides: s = –7S
The answer is (D).
