Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

CONVOLUTED : UNIFORM ::

  1. prosaic : animated

  2. fatuous : apathetic

  3. mild : indulgent

  4. disconsolate : unmistakable

  5. apprehensive : distraught

Solution:CONVOLUTED means complex, intricate, which is the opposite of UNIFORM. Similarly, PROSAIC (flat, uninspired) is the opposite of ANIMATED (spirited, vivacious). The answer is (A).
