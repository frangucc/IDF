Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SHARD : GLASS ::

  1. clump : clay

  2. splinter : wood

  3. crystal : rock

  4. fragment : torch

  5. snippet : sliver

Solution:A SHARD is a sharp piece of GLASS. Likewise, a SPLINTER is a sharp piece of WOOD. The answer is (B).
