Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Line segments AB and CD are both parallel and congruent. The mid point of AB is M.

Column A
The length of segment CM

Column B
The length of segment DM

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

(Note: When drawing a geometric figure, be careful not to assume more than what is given. In this problem, we are told only that the two lines are parallel and congruent; we cannot assume that they are aligned.)
