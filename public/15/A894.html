Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DEVIATE : LECTURE ::

  1. broadcast : information

  2. disown : friend

  3. welcome : indifference

  4. entreat : solicitation

  5. meander : drive

Solution:There is no relationship between DISOWN and FRIEND. One does not expect friends to disown each other eliminate (B). Now, to DEVIATE from the LECTURE is to go off the main topic, to digress. Similarly, to MEANDER is to DRIVE off the main path. The answer is (E).
