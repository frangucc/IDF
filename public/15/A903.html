Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

HEDONIST : UNSTINTING ::

  1. protagonist : insignificant

  2. thug : aggressive

  3. politician : irresolute

  4. benefactor : generous

  5. drunkard : manifest

Solution:GENEROUS is the eye-catcher since it means UNSTINTING.
Hence, HEDONIST and UNSTINTING are antonyms. Similarly, PROTAGONIST (leading character) and INSIGNIFICANT are antonyms. The answer is (A).
