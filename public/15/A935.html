Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PATRIOTIC : NATIONALISTIC ::

  1. prophetic : gratified

  2. hideous : aesthetic

  3. frugal : miserly

  4. sordid : adept

  5. sylvan : inclusive

Solution:NATIONALISM is an extreme form of PATRIOTISM. Similarly, MISERLY is being extremely FRUGAL. The answer is (C).
