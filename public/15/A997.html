Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

EXHORT : ADVISE ::

  1. macerate : astound

  2. assail : engross

  3. persist : perish

  4. galvanize : arouse

  5. blush : supervise

Solution:EXHORT means to ADVISE strongly. Similarly, GALVANIZE means to AROUSE strongly. The answer is (D).
