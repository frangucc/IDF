Q. If the square In the figure below is rotated clock wise about the origin until vertex V is on the negative y-axis, then the new y-coordinate of V is

  1. –2

  2. −2√2

  3. –4

  4. −3√2

  5. –8

Solution: Calculating the distance between V and the origin yields √(2 − 0)2 + (− 2 − 0)2 =  √4 + 4 = √8 = 2√2. Since the square is rotated about the origin, the distance between the origin and V is fix.
Hence, the new y-coordinate of V is −2√2. The diagram below illustrates the position of V after the rotation. The answer is B.
