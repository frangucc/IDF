Q. Look for secondary meanings; then solve.

FLUID:

  1. radiant

  2. smooth

  3. solid

  4. balky

  5. craggy

Solution:Since all the answer-choices are adjectives, FLUID must also be an adjective. Now, as an adjective, FLUID means "moving in a continuous, smooth manner." The opposite would be moving in a hesitating manner, which is the meaning of "balky." (Think of a "balk" in the game of baseball.) The answer is (D).
