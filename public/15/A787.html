Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

EPIGRAMMATIC:

  1. insipid

  2. indiscriminate

  3. poignant

  4. droll

  5. invidious

Solution:EPIGRAMMATIC means "clever, witty." The opposite is INSIPID—dull, prosaic. The answer is (A).
