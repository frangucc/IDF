Q.   The smallest prime number greater than 53 is

  1. 54

  2. 55

  3. 57

  4. 59

  5. 67

Solution:Since the question asks for the smallest prime greater than 53, we start with the smallest answer-choice. 54 is not prime since 54 = 2(27). 55 is not prime since 55 = 5(11). 57 is not prime since 57 = 3(19). Now, 59 is prime.
Hence, the answer is (D).
