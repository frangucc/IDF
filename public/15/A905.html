Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

HECTOR : EQUANIMITY ::

  1. quicken : cognition

  2. harass : contravention

  3. affirm : scruple

  4. deviate : itinerary

  5. maraud : locality

Solution:HARASS is the eye-catcher since it means to HECTOR. Now, HECTOR and EQUANIMITY (calmness of mind) are antonyms. Similarly, AFFIRM (assert) and SCRUPLE (misgiving, doubt) are antonyms. The answer is (C).
