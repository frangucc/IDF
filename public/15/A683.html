Q. Change the capitalized word into a more common form of the word and then find its antonym.

VISCID:

  1. subtle

  2. faint

  3. slick

  4. vicious

  5. difficult

Solution:You have probably never seen the word VISCID, but changing the ending yields viscous or viscosity. The viscosity of oil is a measure of the thickness or gumminess of oil.
Hence, VISCID means thick or gummy, and the opposite of gummy is SLICK. The answer is (C).
