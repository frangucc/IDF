ACTION & SIGNIFICANCE

In this type of analogy one word describes an action and the other word indicates the significance of the action.

Example:
BLUSH : EMBARRASSMENT ::

  1. gesticulate : resentment

  2. frown : disappointment

  3. smirk : anguish

  4. scowl : preference

  5. grimace : tenacity

Solution:BLUSHING signifies EMBARRASSMENT. Similarly, FROWNING signifies DISAPPOINTMENT The answer is (B).

Example:
CURTSY : REVERENCE ::

  1. assume : disguise

  2. bestir : deferment

  3. fret : contentment

  4. forgo : diversion

  5. fidget : uneasiness

Solution:A CURTSY (bow) is a sign of REVERENCE. Similarly, FIDGETING is a sign of UNEASINESS The answer is (E).
