Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SURREPTITIOUS : STEALTH ::

  1. clandestine : openness

  2. guarded : effrontery

  3. bombastic : irreverence

  4. pernicious : bane

  5. impertinent : humility

Solution:The eye-catcher is (A): CLANDESTINE has the same meaning as SURREPTITIOUS. Now, SURREPTITIOUS means secretive or STEALTHY. Similarly, PERNICIOUS means injurious or BANEFUL. The answer is (D).
