Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Cars X and Y leave City A at the same time and travel the same route to City B. Car X takes 30 minutes to complete the trip and car Y takes 20 minutes.

Column A
The average miles per hour at which car X traveled

Column B
The average miles per hour at which car Y traveled

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.
