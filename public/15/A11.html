Q. Solve the following problems by using substitution.

If x is an integer, then which of the following is the product of the next two integers greater than 2(x + 1)?

  1. 4x2 + 14x + 12

  2. 4x2 + 12

  3. x2 + 14x + 12

  4. x2 + x + 12

  5. 4x2 + 14x

Solution:Suppose x = 1, an integer. Then 2(x + 1) = 2(1 + 1) = 4. The next two integers greater than 4 are 5 and 6, and their product is 30. Now, check which of the answer-choices equal 30 when x = 1. Begin with (A): 4x2 + 14x + 12 = 4(1)2 + 14 × 1 + 12 = 30. No other answer-choice equals 30 when x = 1.
Hence, the answer is (A).
