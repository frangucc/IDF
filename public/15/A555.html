Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Column A
Arithmetic mean of the numbers: 13, 15, 17, 19, 21

Column B
Arithmetic mean of the numbers: 11, 13, 15, 17, 19, 21, 23

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution:Column A: Since the given series of numbers are in arithmetic progression, the mean will be the middle number in the series. The mean of the series is 17 since it is the middle number.

Column B: Since the given series of numbers are also in arithmetic progression, the mean will be the middle number in the series. The mean of the series is 17 since it is the middle number.

Hence, the values in the columns A and B are same. The answer is (C).
