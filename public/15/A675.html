Q. Change the capitalized word into a more common form of the word and then find its antonym.

VOLUMINOUS:

  1. obscure

  2. cantankerous

  3. unsubstantial

  4. tenacious

  5. opprobrious

Solution:Embedded in the word VOLUMINOUS is the word VOLUME. So we are looking for a word that is related to size. The only answer-choice related to size is UNSUBSTANTIAL. The answer is (C). (VOLUMINOUS means "large.")
