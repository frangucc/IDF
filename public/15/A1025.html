Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DRACONIAN : COERCION ::

  1. insouciant : resplendence

  2. munificent : philanthropy

  3. blatant : concealment

  4. submissive : consequence

  5. spent : existence

Solution:DRACONIAN describes strict and COERCIVE rules. Similarly, MUNIFICENT describes PHILANTHROPIC (charitable) behavior. The answer is (B).
