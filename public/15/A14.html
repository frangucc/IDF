Q. Solve the following problems by using substitution.

Which one of the following is a solution to the equation x4 − 2x2 = −1 ?

  1. 0

  2. 1

  3. 2

  4. 3

  5. 4

Solution:We could solve the equation, but it is much faster to just plug in the answer-choices. Begin with 0:
x4 − 2x2 = 04 − 2 × 02 = 0 − 0 = 0
Hence, eliminate (A).
Next, plug in 1:
x4 − 2x2 = 14 − 2 × 12 = 1 − 2 = −1
Hence, the answer is (B).
