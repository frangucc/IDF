Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x < y < 0

Column A
x + y

Column B
xy

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: Since x < y < 0, both x and y are negative. Now, the sum of two negative numbers is negative.
Hence, x + y is negative.
Next, product of two negative numbers is positive.
Hence, xy is positive.
Therefore, Column B is larger than Column A, and the answer is (B).
