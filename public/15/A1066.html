Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Despite her age, she has a silly and _______ sense of humor.

  1. mature

  2. trivial

  3. adolescent

  4. asinine

  5. youthful

Solution:The word "age" in the sentence implies that the missing word is characteristic of age. "Youthful" fits well: Despite her advanced age, she has a youthful sense of humor. The answer is (E). "Adolescent" could also work in the sentence, but the phrase "adolescent sense of humor" carries a negative connotation, and the sentence does not seem to be critical.
