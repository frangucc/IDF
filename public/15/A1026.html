Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

CLOYED : JADED ::

  1. circumscribed : trifling

  2. insatiable : cormorant

  3. effeminate : conscientious

  4. rectilinear : graceful

  5. penurious : fatuous

Solution:CLOYED (satiated) and JADED are synonyms. Similarly, INSATIABLE and CORMORANT (greedy) are synonyms. The answer is (B).
