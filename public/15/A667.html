Q. Choose the word most opposite in meaning to the capitalized word.

SAVANT:

  1. dolt

  2. sage

  3. attaché

  4. apropos comment

  5. state of confusion

Solution:Think of the description: "Idiot-savant." An idiot-savant is a person who exhibits the characteristics of both a mentally retarded person and a mental gifted person. SAVANT means "reflective thinker." The opposite is a DOLT. The answer is (A).
