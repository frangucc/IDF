Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

GRATIS:

  1. unsatisfactory

  2. gratuitous

  3. baneful

  4. commensurable

  5. extravagant

Solution:GRATIS comes from Latin, and you may also recognize it from the French word gratuit. It means free of charge. EXTRAVAGANT is the opposite of gratis. The answer is (E).
