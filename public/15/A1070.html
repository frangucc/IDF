Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

At the cutting edge of research, scientists are developing new sunscreens of both ________ and internal varieties.

  1. polar

  2. tropical

  3. territorial

  4. atmospheric

  5. regional

Solution:The word "both" in the statement implies there are two different types of sunscreen. Since one is internal, the other one should be external or at least of a different type. The choices "territorial," "atmospheric," and "regional" do not imply the opposite of internal. "Polar" and "tropical" may indicate other varieties. Sunscreens are not used in polar regions; they are used in tropical regions.
Hence, the answer is (B).
