Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MERCURIAL : TEMPERAMENT ::

  1. capricious : interest

  2. tempestuous : solemnity

  3. staid : wantonness

  4. phlegmatic : concern

  5. cynical : naivet

Solution:A MERCURIAL person is one whose TEMPERAMENT changes quickly. Similarly, a CAPRICIOUS person is one whose INTEREST changes quickly. The answer is (A).
