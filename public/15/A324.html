Q. If x is an integer and y = –2x – 8, what is the least value of x for which y is less than 9?

  1. –9

  2. –8

  3. –7

  4. –6

  5. –5

Solution: Since y is less than 9 and y = –2x – 8, we get –2x – 8 < 9

Adding 8 to both sides of this inequality yields –2x < 17

Dividing by –2 and reversing the inequality yields x > –17/2 = –8.5

Since x is an integer and is to be as small as possible, x = –8
The answer is B.
