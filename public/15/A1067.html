Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

There are different and ________ versions about what happened in the city, but one thing is certain: it a dastardly act that must be condemned ________.

  1. dissimilar . . concertedly

  2. contrary . . in unison

  3. unique . . without conflict

  4. conflicting . . unequivocally

  5. complementary . . unanimously

Solution:The word "and" in the phrase "different and ________ versions" indicates that the missing word is similar in meaning to the word "different." Now, different versions of an event can be "conflicting." Further, a dastardly act needs to be condemned "unequivocally." The answer is (D).
