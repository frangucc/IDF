Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

AGNOSTIC:

  1. unfaithful

  2. arcane

  3. heathen

  4. evangelical

  5. mundane

Solution:The root "gnosi" means "knowledge," and the prefix "a" means "without."
Hence, AGNOSTIC means "without knowledge." In this case, without knowledge that God exists. The opposite is EVANGELICAL (devout, crusading). The answer is (D).
