Q.   Tom is 10 years older than Carrie. However, 5 years ago Tom was twice as old as Carrie. How old is Carrie?

  1. 5

  2. 10

  3. 12

  4. 15

  5. 25

Solution:Let C be Carrie’s age. Then Tom’s age is C + 10. Now, 5 years ago, Carrie’s age was C – 5 and Tom’s age was (C + 10) – 5 = C + 5. Since at that time, Tom was twice as old as Carrie, we get 5 + C = 2(C – 5). Solving this equation for C yields C = 15. The answer is (D).
