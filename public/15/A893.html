Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

EMPATHY : FEELING ::

  1. melancholy : joy

  2. sibling : relative

  3. Spartan : wickedness

  4. boldness : guilt

  5. institution : encouragement

Solution:SPARTAN has little to do with WICKEDNESS. So eliminate (C). Also BOLDNESS has little to do with GUILT. Eliminate (D).

Now, EMPATHY is a type of FEELING. MEL-ANCHOLY is not a type of JOY eliminate (A). And INSTITUTION is not a type of ENCOURAGEMENT eliminate (E). However, a SIBLING is a type of RELATIVE. The answer is (B).
