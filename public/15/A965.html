Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

GREGARIOUS : CONGENIAL ::

  1. suspicious : trusting

  2. pedantic : lively

  3. bellicose : militant

  4. singular : nondescript

  5. seminal : apocalyptic

Solution:GREGARIOUS and CONGENIAL are synonyms"both mean "sociable, friendly. "Likewise, BELLICOSE and MILITANT are synonyms"both mean "aggressive, warlike. "All the other answer-choice pairs contain antonyms. The answer is (C).
