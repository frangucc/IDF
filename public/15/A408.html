Q.   The average of two numbers is π/2, and one of the numbers is x. What is the other number in terms
of x ?

  1.  π 
     2 
     − x

  2.  π 
     2 
     + x

  3. π – x

  4. π + x

  5. 2π + x

Solution:Let the other number be y. Since the average of the two numbers is π/2, we get

 x + y 
 2 
 = 
 π 
 2 

Multiplying both sides of this equation by 2 yields x + y = π

Subtracting x from both sides of this equation yields y = π – x
The answer is (C).
