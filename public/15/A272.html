Q. If the circumference of a circle is 4m, then the ratio of circumference of the circle to the diameter of the circle is

  1. π

  2. 4

  3. 2π

  4. 4π

  5. 16

Solution:The formula for the circumference of a circle with diameter d is C = 2πr = π(2r) = πd (since the diameter is twice the radius, d = 2r).
Hence, the ratio of the circumference of the circle to its diameter is

 C 
 d 
=

 nd 
 d 
 =

π

The answer is A.


Note: The fact that the circumference of the circle is 4m was not used in solving the problem. Thus, the answer is independent of the size of the circle. In other words, the ratio of the circumference of a circle to its diameter is always π.
