Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DRONE : EMOTION ::

  1. sprint : journey

  2. annoy : emollient

  3. stupefy : erudition

  4. deadpan : expression

  5. scuttle : ship

Solution:To DRONE is to speak in a monotonous, UNEMOTIONAL manner. Similarly, to DEADPAN is to speak or respond in an EXPRESSIONLESS manner. The answer is (D).
