Q. Choose the word most opposite in meaning to the capitalized word.

METE:

  1. indict

  2. convoke

  3. hamper

  4. disseminate

  5. deviate

Solution:Think of the phrase: "to mete out justice." METE means "to dispense, to distribute." The opposite is to gather, which is the meaning of CONVOKE. The answer is (B).
