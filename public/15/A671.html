Q. Choose the word most opposite in meaning to the capitalized word.

REQUIEM:

  1. humility

  2. prerequisite

  3. resolution

  4. reign

  5. hiatus

Solution:Think of the phrase: "Requiem for a heavyweight." REQUIEM means "a rest from an arduous task." The opposite is REIGN, the time spent in power or at the top. The answer is (D).
