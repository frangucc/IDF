Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

QUIXOTIC : PRAGMATIC ::

  1. romantic : fanciful

  2. dispassionate : just

  3. auspicious : sanguine

  4. malcontent : jingoistic

  5. optimistic : surreal

Solution:QUIXOTIC and PRAGMATIC are antonyms. QUIXOTIC means "romantic, idealistic," whereas PRAGMATIC means "practical, realistic, utilitarian." Likewise, MALCONTENT and JINGOISTIC are antonyms. MALCONTENT means "dissatisfied, complaining," whereas JINGOISTIC means "zealous commitment, chauvinistic." The answer is (D).
