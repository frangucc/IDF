Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

THIMBLE : FINGER ::

  1. glove : hammer

  2. stitch : loop

  3. branch : flower

  4. talon : eagle

  5. smock : apparel

Solution:A THIMBLE functions to protect the FINGER. Similarly, a SMOCK functions to protect APPAREL. The answer is (E).
