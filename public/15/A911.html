Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

ANATHEMA : CURSE ::

  1. hex : blessing

  2. admonition : censure

  3. incantation : discernment

  4. theory : calculation

  5. conjecture : truth

Solution:ANATHEMA and CURSE are synonyms. An ANATHEMA is an ecclesiastic curse. Choice (A) is an eye-catcher since HEX reminds one of a CURSE. Eliminate (A). ADMONITION and CENSURE are synonyms; both mean an expression of warning or disapproval. However, CENSURE is much stronger; it both warns and denounces. The answer is (B).
