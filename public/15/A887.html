Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DISQUIETUDE : ANXIOUS ::

  1. magnitude : unabridged

  2. isolation : sequestered

  3. cupidity : bellicose

  4. embellishment : overstated

  5. nonplus : perplexed

Solution:DISQUIETUDE is ANXIETY. Similarly, NONPLUS is PERPLEXITY. The answer is (E).

Choice (B) is a close second-best. SEQUESTERED does mean ISOLATED. However, the pair NONPLUS and PERPLEXED offers a stronger relationship because both words describe a mental state, as do both words of the pair DISQUIETUDE and ANXIOUS.
