Q.   If Robert can assemble a model car in 30 minutes and Craig can assemble the same model car in 20 minutes, how long would it take them, working together, to assemble the model car?

  1. 12 minutes

  2. 13 minutes

  3. 14 minutes

  4. 15 minutes

  5. 16 minutes

Solution:Let t be the time it takes the boys, working together, to assemble the model car. Then their combined rate is 1/t, and their individual rates are 1/30 and 1/20. Now, their combined rate is merely the sum of their individual rates:

 1 
 t 
 = 
 1 
 30 
 + 
 1 
 20 

Solving this equation for t yields t = 12. The answer is (A).
