In 1992, the profit from copying for corporate customers was approximately how much greater than the profit from copying for government customers?

  1. 50 thousand

  2. 80 thousand

  3. 105 thousand

  4. 190 thousand

  5. 260 thousand

Solution:From the chart, the profit in 1992 for copying was approximately $340,000 of which 35% x $340,000 = $119,000 was from corporate customers and 20% x $340,000 = $68,000 was from government customers. Subtracting these amounts yields

$119,000 – $68,000 = $51,000
The answer is (A).
