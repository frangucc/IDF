Q. Eliminate the eye-catchers in the following; then solve.

SEEMLY:

  1. redoubtable

  2. flaccid

  3. imperceptible

  4. indigenous

  5. unbecoming

Solution:SEEMLY appears to mean "seem," and the opposite of "seem" is "imperceptible." However, "imperceptible" is an eye-catcher. The actual meaning of SEEMLY is "appropriate or proper." You may be more familiar with its antonym "unseemly." The answer is (E), UNBECOMING.
