Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

COWER : FEAR ::

  1. slouch : exhilaration

  2. strut : self-confidence

  3. stiffen : dubiety

  4. quake : avail

  5. slumber : vigilance

Solution:COWERING is a sign of FEAR. Similarly, STRUTTING is a sign of SELF-CONFIDENCE. The answer is (B).
