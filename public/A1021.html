Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

POLITIC : PRUDENT ::

  1. portentous : contemptible

  2. exquisite : unrefined

  3. insulting : reputable

  4. disconcerting : embarrassing

  5. bucolic : constrictive

Solution:POLITIC and PRUDENT are synonyms. Similarly, DISCONCERTING and EMBARRASSING are synonyms. The answer is (D).
