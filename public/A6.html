Q. Solve the following problems by using substitution.

If m is an even integer, then which of the following is the sum of the next two even integers greater than 4m + 1?

  1. 8m + 2

  2. 8m + 4

  3. 8m + 6

  4. 8m + 8

  5. 8m + 10

Solution:Suppose m = 2, an even integer. Then 4m + 1 = 9, which is odd.
Hence, the next even integer greater than 9 is 10. And the next even integer after 10 is 12. Now, 10 + 12 = 22. So look for an answer-choice which equals 22 when m = 2.

Begin with choice (A). Since m = 2, 8m + 2 = 18—eliminate (A).
Next, 8m + 4 = 20—eliminate (B).
Next, 8m + 6 = 22.
Hence, the answer is (C).
