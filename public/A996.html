Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

BRAVE : RASH ::

  1. mundane : impulsive

  2. narcotic : marvelous

  3. accommodating : fawning

  4. glib : reticent

  5. gauche : deft

Solution:RASH means BRAVE, but is carries the negative connotation of being foolhardy. Similarly, FAWNING means ACCOMMODATING, but it carries the negative connotation of groveling. The answer is (C).
