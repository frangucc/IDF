Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

PROTÉGÉ:

  1. prodigy

  2. pedagogue

  3. liegeman

  4. prodigal

  5. imbecile

Solution:PROTÉGÉ comes from the French word protéger, which means to protect. A protégé is protected by a mentor, which is the opposite of protégé. One type of mentor is a teacher or PEDAGOGUE. The answer is (B).
