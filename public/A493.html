SALES BY CATEGORY FOR GRAMMERCY PRESS, 1980–1989

(in thousands of books)

What was the approximate average number of sales of fiction titles from 1984 to 1988?

  1. 15 thousand

  2. 30 thousand

  3. 40 thousand

  4. 48 thousand

  5. 60 thousand

Solution:The following chart summarizes the sales for the years 1984 to 1988:

Year

Sales

1984

30 thousand

1985

11 thousand

1986

52 thousand

1987

52 thousand

1988

95 thousand

Forming the average yields:

  30 + 11 + 52 + 52 + 95  
 5 
 = 48
The answer is (D).

Note, it is important to develop a feel for how the writers of the GRE approximate when calculating. We used 52 thousand to calculate the sales of fiction in 1986, which is the actual number. But from the chart, it is difficult to tell whether the actual number is 51, 52, or 53 thousand. However, using any of the these numbers, the average would still be nearer to 40 than to any other answer-choice.
