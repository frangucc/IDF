Q.   A web press prints 5 pages every 2 seconds. At this rate, how many pages will the press print in 7 minutes?

  1. 350

  2. 540

  3. 700

  4. 950

  5. 1050

Solution:Since there are 60 seconds in a minute and the press prints 5 pages every 2 seconds, the press prints 5 × 30 = 150 pages in one minute.
Hence, in 7 minutes, the press will print 7 × 150 = 1050  pages. The answer is (E).
