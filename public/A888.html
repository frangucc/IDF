Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MILK : DRAIN ::

  1. insult : commend

  2. abstract : distend

  3. extend : disregard

  4. exploit : employ

  5. assail : rescind

Solution:To MILK something is to DRAIN it thoroughly. Think of the phrase, To milk it for all it s worth. Similarly, to EXPLOIT something is to EMPLOY it thoroughly, to take full advantage of it. The answer is (D).
