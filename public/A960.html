Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MAROON : SEQUESTER ::

  1. transfix : emote

  2. exhaust : innervate

  3. tranquilize : qualify

  4. select : rebuff

  5. entreat : beseech

Solution:Both MAROON and SEQUESTER mean "to isolate, to segregate."
Hence, we are looking for synonyms. Now, both ENTREAT and BESEECH mean "to ask, to plead."
Hence, the answer is (E).
