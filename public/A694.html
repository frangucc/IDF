Q. Eliminate the eye-catchers in the following; then solve.

UPSHOT:

  1. consequence

  2. descent

  3. annihilation

  4. termination

  5. inception

Solution:The eye-catcher is "descent." UPSHOT contains UP and the opposite of up is down, or a descent. Now, UPSHOT is a result. Whereas, an inception is the beginning of something, it does not follow anything. The answer is (E).
