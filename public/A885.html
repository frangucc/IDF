Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SENTENCE : WORDS ::

  1. album : guitar

  2. manuscript : editor

  3. paragraph : punctuation

  4. novel : index

  5. collage : paper strips

Solution:Paraphrase: A SENTENCE is made of WORDS. Now, an ALBUM is not made of a GUITAR. A MANUSCRIPT is not made of an EDITOR. A PARAGRAPH is not made of PUNCTUATION. A NOVEL is not made of an INDEX. However, a COLLAGE is made of PAPER STRIPS. The answer is (E).
