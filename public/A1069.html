Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Behind their strange appearance and ________ for carrion, which has long singled them out for fear and loathing, hyenas present a ________ society in which females dominate.

  1. longing . . contrastive

  2. penchant . . realistic

  3. proclivity . . virtual

  4. appetite . . matriarchal

  5. yearning . . monarchal

Solution:Since hyenas eat carrion (decaying flesh), they have an "appetite" for it. A society ruled by females is called "matriarchal." The answer is (D).
