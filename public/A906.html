Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

RUSE : TRICK ::

  1. procrastination : delay

  2. conference : perpetuate

  3. hoax : murmur

  4. radiation : worship

  5. obliteration : embellish

Solution:HOAX is the eye-catcher since it means a RUSE. Now, the purpose of a RUSE is to TRICK Similarly, the purpose of PROCRASTINATING is to DELAY. The answer is (A).
