Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Some are born with a _______ to commit suicide, whereas some commit suicide because they are unable to bear _______ changes in their lives.

  1. sentiment . . inimical

  2. resolution. . adverse

  3. predisposition. . cataclysmic

  4. prognosis. . miserable

  5. prodigy. . abrupt

Solution:The author states that some people are born with an inclination to commit suicide. Though the remaining people who have committed suicide do not have such a predisposition, they commit it because they are unable to bear sudden cataclysmic changes that occur in their lives. The answer is (C).
