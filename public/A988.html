Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

BRACKETS : ENCLOSE ::

  1. hyphen : join

  2. comma : introduce

  3. semicolon : list

  4. braces : exclude

  5. underline : de-emphasize

Solution:Like parentheses, BRACKETS ([]) ENCLOSE words or items. Similarly, a HYPHEN JOINS words or parts of words. The answer is (A).
