Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

DISCERNING : MISAPPREHENSION ::

  1. phlegmatic : alacrity

  2. gracious : passion

  3. copious : adequacy

  4. pompous : rudeness

  5. brusque : comity

Solution:DISCERNING means keen, perspicacious, which is the opposite of MISAPPREHENSION (misunderstanding). Similarly, BRUSQUE (curt, rude) is the opposite of COMITY (gracious). The answer is (E).
