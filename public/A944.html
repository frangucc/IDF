Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SECLUSION : HERMIT ::

  1. wealth : embezzler

  2. ambition : philanthropist

  3. domination : athlete

  4. turpitude : introvert

  5. injustice : lawyer

Solution:By definition, a HERMIT desires SECLUSION. Likewise, an EMBEZZLER desires WEALTH. The answer is (A).
