Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

The sum of the three numbers in each diagonal is the same.

Column A
x

Column B
z

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: Since the sum of the three numbers in each diagonal is the same, we get

x + y + 3 = z + y + 7

Subtracting y and 3 from both sides of this equation yields

x = z + 4

This equation says that 4 must be added to z to make it as large as x.
Hence, x is larger than z. Thus, Column A is larger, and the answer is (A).
