Q.   If 4 percent of (p + q) is 8 and p is a positive integer, what is the greatest possible value of q?

  1. 196

  2. 197

  3. 198

  4. 199

  5. 200

Solution:Translating the clause "4 percent of (p + q) is 8" into a mathematical expression yields

.04(p + q) = 8

Dividing both sides of this equation by .04 yields

p + q = 8/.04 = 200

Subtracting p from both sides yields

q = 200 – p

This expression will be greatest when p is as small as possible. This is when p = 1:

q = 200 – 1 = 199
The answer is (D).
