Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Although the buildings and streets of this small beach town appear               , the property values are quite               .

  1. expensive . . steep

  2. dilapidated . . high

  3. artistic . . pedestrian

  4. refurbished . . low

  5. quaint . . reasonable

Solution:"Although" sets up a contrast between what the property values are (high) and what one would expect them to be in a dilapidated (run down) community. The answer is (B).
