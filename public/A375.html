Q.   k is a constant in the equation
 u − v 
 k 
 = 8 . If u = 18 when v = 2, then what is the value of u when v = 4?

  1. –3

  2. 0

  3. 10

  4. 23/2

  5. 20

Solution:Substituting u = 18 and v = 2 into the equation
 u − v 
 k 
 = 8 gives
 18 − 2 
 k 
 = 8

Subtracting gives 16/k = 8

Multiplying both sides of this equation by k gives 16 = 8k

Dividing by 8 gives 2 = k

With this value for k, the original equation becomes
 u − v 
 2 
 = 8

Now, we are asked to find u when v = 4.

Replacing v with 4 in the equation
 u − v 
 2 
 = 8 gives
 u − 4 
 2 
 = 8

Multiplying by 2 gives u – 4 = 16

Adding 4 gives u = 20

Hence, the answer is (E).
