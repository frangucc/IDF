Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PROSTRATE : DEFERENCE ::

  1. smile : pleasure

  2. lurch : irreverence

  3. demur : concession

  4. aver : indulgence

  5. accept : hardship

Solution:Lying PROSTRATE (face to the ground) is a sign of DEFERENCE (courteous respect). Similarly, SMILING is a sign of PLEASURE. The answer is (A).
