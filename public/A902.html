Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

LEVEE : RIVER ::

  1. rampart : barrier

  2. cordon : throng

  3. broker : investment

  4. promontory : height

  5. string : guitar

Solution:RAMPART is the eye-catcher since it reminds one of a LEVEE. In fact, a LEVEE can be described as a RAMPART both are embankments or barriers. Now, a LEVEE contains a RIVER. Similarly, a CORDON contains a THRONG. (Police cordoned off the crowd.) The answer is (B).
