Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x ≠ 0 and
 |x| 
 x 
 = 1.

Column A
1

Column B
|x|

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution:The expression expression

 = 1 tells us only that x is positive.
Hence, there is not sufficient information to answer the question. For example, if x = 1, then
 |x| 
 x 
 = 
 |1| 
 1 
 = 
 1 
 1 
 = 1 and |x| = 1 × 1. However, if x = 2, then

 |2| 
 2 
 = 
 2 
 2 
 = 1 and |x| = 2 > 1. The answer is (D).
