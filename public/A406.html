Q.   The average of four numbers is 20. If one of the numbers is removed, the average of the remaining numbers is 15. What number was removed?

  1. 10

  2. 15

  3. 30

  4. 35

  5. 45

Solution:Let the four numbers be a, b, c, and d. Since their average is 20, we get
 a + b + c + d 
 4 
 = 20.

Let d be the number that is removed. Since the average of the remaining numbers is 15, we get

 a + b + c 
 3 
 = 15

Solving for a + b + c yields a + b + c = 45

Substituting this into the first equation yields
 45 + d 
 4 
 = 20

Multiplying both sides of this equation by 4 yields 45 + d = 80

Subtracting 45 from both sides of this equation yields d = 35
The answer is (D).
