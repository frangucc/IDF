Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

BENIGN : PERNICIOUS ::

  1. ostentatious : tawdry

  2. mortified : nefarious

  3. apocryphal : categorical

  4. discerning : keen

  5. pejorative : vicarious

Solution:BENIGN means "harmless, good," and PERNICIOUS means "harmful, evil."
Hence, we are looking for antonyms. Now, APOCRYPHAL means "of doubtful authenticity, false," whereas CATEGORICAL means "certain, without doubt."
Hence, the answer is (C).
