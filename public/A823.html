Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

INTREPID:

  1. tremulous

  2. viable

  3. gallant

  4. jocular

  5. invidious

Solution:The prefix "in" means "not," and the root "trep" means "fear."
Hence, INTREPID means "fearless." The opposite is "TREMULOUS." The answer is (A).
