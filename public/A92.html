Q.   If the product of two integers is odd, then the sum of those two integers must be

  1. odd

  2. even

  3. prime

  4. divisible by the difference of the two numbers

  5. a perfect square

Solution:If the product of the two numbers is odd, then each number in the product must be odd. Recall that the sum of two odd numbers is an even number. The answer is (B).
