Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PRONE : PREDESTINED ::

  1. genuine : sanctimonious

  2. sedate : flighty

  3. probable : doubtful

  4. noteworthy : celebrated

  5. developed : impotent

Solution:Something PREDESTINED is very, or certain PRONE to occur. Similarly, something CELEBRATED is very NOTEWORTHY. The answer is (D).
