Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PURGE : OPPONENT ::

  1. entrench : comrade

  2. elevate : criminal

  3. liquidate : politician

  4. desalinize : salt

  5. assuage : reactionary

Solution:In politics, to PURGE an OPPONENT is to remove him or her from office, often violently. Similarly, to DESALINIZE is to remove SALT from sea water. The answer is (D).
