Q.   If n is an integer, then which one of the following expressions must be even?

  1. n2 + 1

  2. n(n + 2)

  3. n(n + 1)

  4. n(n + 4)

  5. (n + 1)(n + 3)

Solution:Answer-choice (C) consists of the product of two consecutive integers. Now, of any two consecutive integers, one of the integers must be even.
Hence, their product must be even. The answer is (C).
