Q. Choose the word most opposite in meaning to the capitalized word.

PRODIGAL:

  1. bountiful

  2. dependent

  3. provident

  4. superfluous

  5. profligate

Solution:Think of the description: "Prodigal son." The prodigal son is the wasteful, spoiled son—a playboy.
Hence, PRODIGAL means "immoderate." The opposite is PROVIDENT—frugal, careful. The answer is (C).
