The number of accidents in 1993 was approximately what percentage of the number of cars?

  1. 1%

  2. 1.5%

  3. 3%

  4. 5%

  5. 10%

Solution:From the charts, the number of accidents in 1993 was 360,000 and the number of cars was 11,000,000. Forming the percentage yields

 360,000 
 11,000,000 
 ≈ 3%
The answer is (C).
