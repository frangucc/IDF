Q. The area of the Triangle ABC shown in the figure is 30. The area of Triangle ADC is

  1. 5

  2. 10

  3. 15

  4. 20

  5. 25

Solution:Let’s add an altitude to Triangle ABC by extending side BC as shown in the figure below.

The formula for the area of a triangle is A = (1/2)(base)(height).
Hence, the area of Triangle ABC = (1/2)(BC)(AF) = (1/2)(2 + 1)(AF) = (3/2)(AF) = 30 (the area of Triangle ABC is given to be 30). Solving this equation for AF yields AF = 20. Now, the area of Triangle ADC = (1/2)(DC)(AF) = (1/2)(1)(20) = 10. The answer is B.
