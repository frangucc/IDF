Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

Column A
The area of the square

Column B
18

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: Let x be the length of the square’s sides. Since the figure is a square, the triangle in the figure is a right triangle and the Pythagorean Theorem applies:

x2 + x2 = 62

      2x2 = 36

        x2 = 18

Hence, the area of the square is 18, and the answer is (C).
