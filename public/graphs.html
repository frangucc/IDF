2. During what two-year period did the company’s earnings increase the greatest?

  1. 85–87

  2. 86–87

  3. 86–88

  4. 87–89

  5. 88–90

Solution:Reading from the graph, we see that the company’s earnings increased from $5 million in 1986 to $10 million in 1987, and then to $12 million in 1988. The two-year increase from ’86 to ’88 was $7 million—clearly the largest on the graph. The answer is (C).
