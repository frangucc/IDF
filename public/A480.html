In 1993, the total profit was approximately how much greater than the total profit in 1990?

  1. 50 thousand

  2. 75 thousand

  3. 120 thousand

  4. 200 thousand

  5. 350 thousand

Solution:Remember, rarely does a graph question involve significant computation. For this question, we need merely to read the bar graph. The Total Profit graph shows that in 1993 approximately 680 thousand was earned, and in 1990 approximately 560 thousand was earned. Subtracting these numbers yields

680 – 560 = 120
The answer is (C).
