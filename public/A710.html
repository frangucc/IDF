Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

BLARNEY:

  1. eloquence

  2. loquacity

  3. volubleness

  4. taciturnity

  5. efficacy

Solution:Legend has it that if you kiss a magical stone in Blarney, Ireland, you will be given the gift of flattering speech, or eloquence. The opposite of BLARNEY, then, is TACITURNITY, which means silence or reticence. The answer is (D).
