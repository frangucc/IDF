Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PANDEMONIUM : EQUANIMITY ::

  1. convention : predicament

  2. anxiety : confidence

  3. aplomb : serenity

  4. superfluity : redundancy

  5. mechanism : contrivance

Solution:PANDEMONIUM means confusion, agitation, which is the opposite of EQUANIMITY (calm, collected). Similarly, ANXIETY is the opposite of CONFIDENCE. The answer is (B).
