Q. In the parallelogram below, ∠BAD + ∠BCD = 140. What is the measure of ∠ABC ?

  1. 100

  2. 110

  3. 120

  4. 125

  5. 142

Solution:Since opposite angles of a parallelogram are equal, ∠ABC = ∠ADC. Further, since there are 360˚ in a parallelogram,

∠ABC + ∠ADC + ∠BAD + ∠BCD = 360

∠ABC + ∠ADC + 140 = 360

∠ABC + ∠ABC = 220

2∠ABC = 220

∠ABC = 110
The answer is B.
