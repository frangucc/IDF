Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

In the 1950s, integration was               to most Americans; now, however, most Americans accept it as               .

  1. welcome . . normal

  2. an anathema . . desirable

  3. voluntary . . mandatory

  4. common . . sporadic

  5. an abhorrence. . unusual

Solution:The sentence is pointing out the difference between the attitudes of people in the ’50s and the attitudes today. The answer is (B).
