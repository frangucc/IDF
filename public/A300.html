Q. In the figure below, a is the x-coordinate of point P and b is the y-coordinate of point Q. In which quadrant is the point (a, b) ?

  1. I

  2. II

  3. III

  4. IV

  5. cannot be determined from the information given

Solution:Since P is in Quadrant II, its x-coordinate is negative. That is, a is negative. Since Q is in Quadrant IV, its y-coordinate is negative. That is, b is negative.
Hence, (a, b) is in Quadrant III. The answer is C.
