Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

LOQUACIOUS : GARRULOUS ::

  1. harsh : kindly

  2. animate : weary

  3. gluttonous : disloyal

  4. rash : impetuous

  5. blithe : gloomy

Solution:LOQUACIOUS and GARRULOUS are synonyms, both mean "talkative. Now HARSH and KINDLY are antonyms. Eliminate (A). ANIMATE (vital, alive) and WEARY are also antonyms. Eliminate (B). GLUTTONOUS and DISLOYAL are not related. Eliminate (C). RASH and IMPETUOUS are synonyms, both mean "hasty." The answer is (D).
