Q. In the figure, if x = 54˚ and y = 72˚, then z =

  1. 54˚

  2. 56˚

  3. 72˚

  4. 76˚

  5. 98˚

Solution:From the figure, observe that ∠AOC and ∠BOD are vertical angles between the lines AB and CD.
Hence, ∠AOC = ∠BOD = x. Since a straight angle has 180˚, we get the following equation:

∠EOD + ∠BOD + ∠BOF = 180

since ∠EOD = z, ∠BOD = x, ∠BOF = y
z + x + y = 180

since x = 54˚ and y = 72˚
z + 54 + 72 = 180

z = 180 – 54 – 72 = 54
The answer is A.
