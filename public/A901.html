Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PECCADILLO : FLAW ::

  1. mediator : dispute

  2. grammar : error

  3. nick : score

  4. forensics : judiciary

  5. invasion : putsch

Solution:ERROR is the eye-catcher since it reminds one of a FLAW. Now, a PECCADILLO is a minor character FLAW. Similarly, a NICK can be interpreted as a minor or small SCORE (a groove in metal or other material that facilitates bending). The answer is (C).
