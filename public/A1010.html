Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SEDULOUS : INDUSTRIOUS ::

  1. axiomatic : perfidious

  2. heedless : irresolute

  3. contrite : culpable

  4. accustomed : patrician

  5. impecunious : destitute

Solution:SEDULOUS and INDUSTRIOUS are synonyms, both mean hardworking. Likewise, IMPECUNIOUS and DESTITUTE are synonyms, both mean poor. The answer is (E).
