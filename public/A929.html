Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

WILLFUL : CONTUMACIOUS ::

  1. convincing : unadorned

  2. grim : tractable

  3. volatile : capricious

  4. manifest : affluent

  5. wanton : sedate

Solution:WILLFUL and CONTUMACIOUS are synonyms"both mean stubborn. Similarly, VOLATILE and CAPRICIOUS are synonyms"both mean erratic. The answer is (C).
