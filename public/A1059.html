Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Although prices               during the fuel shortage, the suppliers actually saw               in profits.

  1. increased . . a loss

  2. stabilized . . a boon

  3. shot up . . an expansion

  4. fluctuated . . a deprivation

  5. decreased . . a windfall

Solution:The sentence is pointing out that in spite of the higher prices the suppliers lost money. The answer is (A).
