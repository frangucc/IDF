Q. If r > t and r < 1 and rt = 1, then which one of the following must be true?

  1. r > 0 and t < –1

  2. r > –1 and t < –1

  3. r < –1 and t > –1

  4. r < 1 and t > 1

  5. r > 1 and t < 0

Solution: Note that the product of r and t is 1. The product of two numbers is positive only if both numbers are positive or both numbers are negative.
Since rt = 1 and r > t, there are two possibilities:

Case I (both negative): –1 < r < 0 and t < –1

Case II (both positive): 0 < t < 1 and r > 1

The second case violates the condition r < 1.
Hence, Case I is true, and the answer is B.
