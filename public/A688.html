Q. Solve the following by checking for positive and negative connotations.

ADULATION:

  1. immutability

  2. reluctance

  3. reflection

  4. defamation

  5. indifference

Solution:ADULATION has a positive connotation. "Immutability," "reluctance," "reflection," and "indifference" are all neutral in connotation—eliminate. Thus, by process of elimination, the answer is (D), "defamation." ADULATION means "praise, applause."
