Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

RECTILINEAR:

  1. circuitous

  2. tawdry

  3. overweening

  4. inimical

  5. brackish

Solution:The root "rect" means "in," and the root "lin" means "line."
Hence, RECTILINEAR means "in a straight line." The opposite is CIRCUITOUS (roundabout, winding). The answer is (A).
