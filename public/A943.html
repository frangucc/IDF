Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

TRANQUILIZER : CALM ::

  1. anodyne : solace

  2. anesthetic : alleviate

  3. opiate : adulterate

  4. acupuncture : contamination

  5. placebo : spasm

Solution:A TRANQUILIZER CALMS a person's mode. Likewise, an ANESTHETIC ALLEVIATES a person's pain. The answer is (B).
