Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Despite its _______ and safety in treating some of the most incapacitating forms of depression and anxiety, it has not been widely _______.

  1. security . . renounced

  2. potency . . repudiated

  3. ineffectuality . . overtaken

  4. productivity . . commenced

  5. usefulness . . accepted

Solution:The conjunction "and" in the phrase "Despite its _______ and safety" indicates that the missing word has a positive meaning because "safety" has a positive meaning. Since the sentence is implying that the drug is useful in curing depression and anxiety, it is expected that the drug would be widely used. But "despite" implies that the drug is not widely "accepted." The answer is (E).
