If no car in 1993 was involved in more than four accidents, what is the minimum number of cars that could have been in accidents in 1993?

  1. 50 thousand

  2. 60 thousand

  3. 70 thousand

  4. 80 thousand

  5. 90 thousand

Solution:The number of cars involved in accidents will be minimized when each car has exactly 4 accidents. Now, from the top chart, there were 360,000 accidents in 1993. Dividing 360,000 by 4 yields

 360,000 
 4 
 = 90,000
The answer is (E).
