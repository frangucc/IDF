Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

The vigorous dispute over where to place a comma in the Republican platform was motivated not by any               change in meaning but by a desire not to show any deference to the other side.

  1. specific

  2. discredited

  3. tarnished

  4. petulant

  5. infinite

Solution:The clause "a desire not to show any defer ence to the other side" implies that the issue was who would win not who was right. So the placement of the comma did not affect the spe cific meaning of the sentence. The answer is (A).
