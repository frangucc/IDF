Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MISOGYNIST : WOMEN ::

  1. despot : citizens

  2. xenophobe : foreigners

  3. sentinel : scions

  4. patriarch : curmudgeons

  5. libertine : officers

Solution:A MISOGYNIST hates WOMEN. Similarly, A XENOPHOBE hates FOREIGNERS. The answer is (B).
