Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PARAGRAPH : ESSAY ::

  1. trailer : automobile

  2. query : question

  3. instrument : surgery

  4. penmanship : essay

  5. shot : salvo

Solution:A PARAGRAPH is part of an ESSAY. Likewise, a SHOT is part of a SALVO (a sequence of shots). The answer is (E).
