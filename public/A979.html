Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

OVERWROUGHT : FRANTIC ::

  1. universal : extraordinary

  2. satiated : full

  3. courageous : craven

  4. rotund : angular

  5. improvident : thoughtful

Solution:OVERWROUGHT means FRANTIC with grief. Similarly, SATIATED means FULL. The answer is (B).
