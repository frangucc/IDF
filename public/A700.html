Q. Look for secondary meanings; then solve.

CURB:

  1. bridle

  2. encourage

  3. reproach

  4. ameliorate

  5. perjure

Solution:Since all the answer-choices are verbs, CURB cannot mean the sidewalk you park your car next to. As a verb, CURB means "to restrain or stop." The opposite of stopping an activity is encouraging it.
Hence, the answer is (B).
