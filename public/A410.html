Q.   The average of 8 numbers is A, and one of the numbers is 14. If 14 is replaced with 28, then what is the new average in terms of A ?

  1. A + 
     7 
     4 

  2. A + 
     1 
     2 

  3. A + 2

  4. 2A + 1

  5. A + 4

Solution:Let the seven unknown numbers be represented by x1, x2,..., x7. Forming the average of the eight numbers yields

 x1 + x2 + … +  x7 + 14 
 8 
 = A

Replacing 14 with 28 (= 14 + 14), and forming the average yields
 x1 + x2 + … +  x7 + (14 + 14) 
 8  

Breaking up the fraction into the sum of two fractions yields
 x1 + x2 + … +  x7 + 14 
 8 
 + 
 14 
 8 

Since
 x1 + x2 + … +  x7 + 14 
 8  
 = A , this becomes A + 
 14 
 8 

Reducing the fraction yields A + 
 7 
 4 

The answer is (A).
