Q. Solve the following by checking for positive and negative connotations.

CONSECRATE:

  1. curb

  2. destroy

  3. curse

  4. inveigh

  5. exculpate

Solution:CONSECRATE (to make holy) has a positive connotation. The only negative word is "curse." The answer is (C).
Note: "Destroy" is neutral, not negative: you can destroy something that is good or bad.
