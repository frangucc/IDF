Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x = y ≠ 0

Column A
0

Column B
x/y

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution:If x and y are positive, then Column B is positive and hence larger than zero. If x and y are negative, then Column B is still positive since a negative divided by a negative yields a positive. This covers all possible signs for x and y. The answer is (B).
