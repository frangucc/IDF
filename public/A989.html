Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

HISTRIONICS : SINCERE ::

  1. existence : virtual

  2. script : genuine

  3. palate : gratifying

  4. score : idealistic

  5. abstract : civil

Solution:HISTRIONICS is exaggerated, insincere behavior, which is the opposite of SINCERE. Similarly, EXISTENCE is the opposite of VIRTUAL (existing in effect but not actual fact). The answer is (A).
