Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Her stern attitude toward the child was complemented with plenty of              .

  1. love

  2. spite

  3. indifference

  4. malice

  5. ambivalence

Solution:A complement is something that makes up a whole, bringing it to perfection. Of the answer-choices offered, only "love" could complement "stern" in such a manner. The answer is (A).
