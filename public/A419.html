Q.   In the system of equations to the right, z ≠ 0. What is ratio of x to z?

x + 2y − z = 1
3x − 2y − 8z = −1

  1. –9/4

  2. –1/3

  3. 1/3

  4. 4/9

  5. 9/4

Solution:This is considered to be a hard problem. Begin by adding the two equations:

x + 2y − z = 1
3x − 2y − 8z = −1
yields
4x – 9z = 0

4x = 9z

 x 
 z 
 = 
 9 
 4 

The answer is (E).
