Q. Eliminate the eye-catchers in the following; then solve.

PRODIGY:

  1. vacuous comment

  2. hegemony

  3. plane

  4. common occurrence

  5. capitulation

Solution:Most people associate a PRODIGY with a mentally gifted child, and the opposite would be stupid. So choice (A), VACUOUS COMMENT is tempting. But this would be too easy a connection for this hard question. Now, a PRODIGY is a person with extraordinary ability or talent, but not necessarily intelligence. By extension, PRODIGY means anything extraordinary. The opposite is a COMMON OCCURRENCE. The answer is (D).
