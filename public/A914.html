Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

INTUITIVE : CONSIDERED ::

  1. impromptu : planning

  2. laborious : safe

  3. ethereal : light

  4. random : sequential

  5. rational : certain

Solution:INTUITIVE means to understand without having to CONSIDER or ponder. Similarly, IMPROMPTU means without PLANNING. The answer is (A).
