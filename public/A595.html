Q.   The average of x, y, and z is 8 and the average of y and z is 4. What is the value of x?

  1. 4

  2. 9

  3. 16

  4. 20

  5. 24

Solution: Recall that the average of N numbers is their sum divided by N. That is, average = sum/N. Since the average of x, y, and z is 8 and the average of y and z is 4, this formula yields

 x + y + z 
 3 
 = 8

      
 y + z 
 2 
 = 4

Solving the bottom equation for y + z yields y + z = 8. Plugging this into the top equation gives

 x + 8 
 3 
 = 8

  x + 8 = 24

         x = 16
The answer is (C).
