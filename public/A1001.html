Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

SOLICITUDE : TACTFUL ::

  1. breadth : deficient

  2. sequestration : ungainly

  3. enterprise : militant

  4. caricature : extravagant

  5. enigma : mysterious

Solution:SOLICITUDE means TACTFUL, thoughtful. Similarly, ENIGMA means MYSTERIOUS, puzzling. The answer is (E).
