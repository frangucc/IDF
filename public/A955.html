Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

NEBULOUS : DISTINCTION ::

  1. guileless : deceit

  2. antipathetic : abhorrence

  3. sublime : disrespect

  4. magnanimous : anxiety

  5. amorphous : inchoation

Solution:NEBULOUS means cloud-like, without form or DISTINCTION. Similarly, GUILELESS means without DECEIT. The answer is (A).

Don"t fall the eye-catcher "amorphous": it reminds one of NEBULOUS. However, "Inchoation" means a beginning, and something just beginning is likely to be amorphous, to lack shape.
