Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

PHILATELICAL : STAMPS ::

  1. nuncupative : rocks

  2. parliamentarian : ships

  3. vigorous : capital

  4. numismatic : currency

  5. geneotypic : viruses

Solution:PHILATELICAL means pertaining to STAMPS. Similarly, NUMISMATIC means pertaining to CURRENCY. The answer is (D).
