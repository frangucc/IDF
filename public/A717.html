Q. Choose the lettered word or phrase that is most nearly opposite in meaning to the word in capital letters.

AMOROUS:

  1. abhorrent

  2. congenial

  3. unadorned

  4. magnanimous

  5. menacing

Solution:You’ve heard the saying "Love in any language." The meaning of AMOROUS clearly relates to love. Recall from your undergraduate classes that love in French is amour, in Spanish amor, and in Italian amore. The opposite of love is hate. ABHORRENT means characteristic of loathing. The answer is (A).
