Q. Complete the sentence with appropriate choice from given choices.

Their courage is only _______, and a small show of strength is enough to call their bluff.

  1. ostentation

  2. fortitude

  3. temperament

  4. exhibition

  5. bravado

Solution:The word "only" in the first clause limits the meaning of courage: They have courage, but only to a point. The word "bluff" in the second clause indicates that their courage is merely bluster. This is the meaning of "bravado." The answer is (E).
