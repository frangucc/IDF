Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

CONTIGUOUS : ABUTTING ::

  1. aphoristic : axiomatic

  2. morbid : salubrious

  3. sanguine : exhilarated

  4. esoteric : enlightened

  5. adventitious : arranged

Solution:CONTIGUOUS and ABUTTING are synonyms, both mean side-by-side, touching. Similarly, APHORISTIC and AXIOMATIC are synonyms, both mean self-evident. The answer is (A).
