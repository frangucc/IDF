Q. Look for secondary meanings; then solve.

DOCUMENT:

  1. copy

  2. implement

  3. gainsay

  4. blanch

  5. rant

Solution:As a noun, DOCUMENT means "a legal or official paper." But all the answer-choices are verbs. As a verb, DOCUMENT means "to attest to, or to supply evidence." The opposite is to contradict, which is the meaning of "gainsay." The answer is (C).
