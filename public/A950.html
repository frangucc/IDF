Q. Choose the lettered pair that best expresses a relationship similar to that expressed in the original pair.

MINCE : EXACERBATE ::

  1. implore : edify

  2. write : foment

  3. decamp : conciliate

  4. chronicle : dispatch

  5. confuse : convince

Solution:To MINCE is to lessen or mitigate (mincing words is using euphemisms), and to EXACERBATE is to worsen.
Hence, we are looking for antonyms. Now, the opposite of CONFUSING someone is to CONVINCE them. The answer is (E).
