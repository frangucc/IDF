Q. Determine which of the two expressions below is larger, whether they are equal, or whether there is not enough information to decide.

x4 = 16
y3 = 8

Column A
x

Column B
y

  1. Column A is larger

  2. Column B is larger

  3. Column A and Column B are equal

  4. Not enough information to decide.

Solution: The fourth roots of 16 are 2, and the cube root of 8 is 2. If we choose the positive fourth root of 16, then the columns are equal. But if we choose the negative fourth root of 16, then column B is larger.
Hence, there is not enough information to decide, and the answer is (D).
